All packets are delivered as PDF files immediately after purchase. You can print them, use them on a tablet, or work through them digitally. No app, no login, no subscription — just the file.
Which packet should my student start with? +
Start with a Level 1 (Foundational) difficulty pack to establish a baseline. This helps identify which topics need the most work, then layer in topic-specific packs from there.
Can I buy SAT and ACT packets in the same order? +
Yes — the mini bundles let you mix and match across SAT and ACT freely. If you're undecided on which test to focus on, this is a great way to compare difficulty and style at a discount.
Is there a money-back guarantee? +
Yes. If you're not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, contact us within 30 days for a full refund — no questions asked.
How are these different from free practice tests? +
Official practice tests are valuable but limited — there are only a handful available, and students often exhaust them quickly. These packets provide fresh, targeted questions organized by topic and difficulty, designed for deliberate practice rather than simulation.
How many questions are in each packet? +
Topic packs contain 50–80 questions. Difficulty packs range from 50 (Elite) to 100 (Foundational). Every packet includes a full answer key, and most include worked solutions.
