Our Story

Built by Students Who Ran Out of Questions

When we were preparing for the SAT and ACT, math was always the bottleneck — not because the concepts were impossible, but because we kept running out of questions to practice on.

Official practice tests come packaged as full exams. That means 30-odd math questions per test, spread across every topic and difficulty level. Burn through the handful of official tests available, and you're out of fresh material — forced to repeat questions you've already seen or settle for low-quality knockoffs.

We built QuantPrep because that problem has a simple fix: more questions. Targeted, high-quality, organized by test — so students can drill what matters, as many times as they need to, without hitting a wall.
